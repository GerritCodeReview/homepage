# Gerrit User Summit 2026 - Speakers

Speakers will be announced as talk proposals are reviewed and accepted.

The **Call for Presentations is open**. To submit a proposal, send a brief abstract and
speaker bio to the [repo-discuss mailing list](https://groups.google.com/g/repo-discuss).

We welcome contributions from:
- Gerrit admins sharing operational experience
- Contributors presenting new features or plugins
- Researchers with findings relevant to code review
- Companies describing how they use or extend Gerrit

Check back here or follow [repo-discuss](https://groups.google.com/g/repo-discuss) for updates.

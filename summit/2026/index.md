# Gerrit User Summit 2026

The Gerrit Community is pleased to announce the **2026 Gerrit User Summit**, hosted at the
[Google offices in Sunnyvale, California](location.html).

The Gerrit User Summit is the event that brings together Gerrit admins, developers, practitioners,
and the whole community in one place, providing attendees with the opportunity to learn, explore,
network face-to-face, and help shape the future of Gerrit development and solutions.

## User Summit

The Gerrit User Summit 2026 will take place in **Sunnyvale, CA** on **November 9-10** and is open
to anyone who wishes to attend, provided they [register to the event](register.html).

Join the User Summit 2026 where the Gerrit community unites for two days of collaboration,
learning, and innovation to drive the future of code review.

## Hackathon

Following the User Summit, a **Gerrit Hackathon** will be hosted by **NVIDIA**.

This is a hands-on session for contributors, plugin developers, and anyone who wants to get
their hands dirty with Gerrit — hacking on core features, plugins, integrations, or anything
the community decides to tackle together. Further details on location and format will be announced on
[repo-discuss](https://groups.google.com/g/repo-discuss).

## Dates

**November 9-10 (Mon - Tue)**

Gerrit User Summit @ Google Sunnyvale

**November 11–13 (Wed–Fri)**

Gerrit Hackathon @ NVIDIA

## Location

The Gerrit User Summit 2026 is hosted at the **Google offices in Sunnyvale, CA**, in cooperation
with [GerritForge Inc.](https://www.gerritforge.com) and the
[Gerrit Community](https://groups.google.com/g/repo-discuss).

See the [Location page](location.html) for address, travel, and venue details.

## Call for Presentations

We welcome talk proposals from the entire Gerrit community. Whether you are a Gerrit admin, a
contributor, or a user with an interesting story to tell, we'd love to hear from you.

The **Call for Presentations is now open**. To submit a proposal, send a brief abstract and
speaker bio to the [repo-discuss mailing list](https://groups.google.com/g/repo-discuss).

      <h3>By Air</h3>
    </div>
    <p>Two airports serve the Sunnyvale area:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Norman Y. Mineta San José International (SJC)</strong> — approximately 10 min drive. Served by most major US carriers. VTA light rail connects the airport to downtown San Jose; onward Caltrain to Sunnyvale.</li>
      <li><strong>San Francisco International (SFO)</strong> — approximately 40 min drive (traffic-dependent). BART connects SFO to Millbrae where you can transfer to Caltrain southbound to Sunnyvale.</li>
      <li><strong>Oakland International (OAK)</strong> — approximately 45 min drive. BART connects OAK to Fremont; from there, take BART to Millbrae and Caltrain south.</li>
    </ul>

## Check-in Instructions

Check-in instructions and badge pick-up details will be communicated to registered attendees before the event.

## Facility Resources

**Food and Drink**

Light breakfast, lunch, and coffee breaks will be provided on both days.

**Internet**

Wi-Fi will be available at the venue. Connection details will be shared on-site.

## Hotels and Places to Stay

Sunnyvale offers a wide range of accommodation options within walking distance or a short drive
of the Google offices. Recommended hotels and a group rate (if available) will be announced
on the [repo-discuss mailing list](https://groups.google.com/g/repo-discuss).

# Gerrit User Summit 2026 - Location

## Hosted at Google Sunnyvale

The User Summit is hosted at the **Google offices in Sunnyvale, California**.

Full address and building details will be shared with registered attendees ahead of the event.

**Sunnyvale, CA**
United States

## Getting There

**By car:** Sunnyvale is easily accessible from US-101 and CA-85. Parking details will be
provided to registered attendees.

**By public transit:** The closest Caltrain station is Sunnyvale Station, with onward connections
by VTA bus. Details TBC.

**By air:** The nearest major airports are:
- Norman Y. Mineta San José International Airport (SJC) — ~10 min drive
- San Francisco International Airport (SFO) — ~40 min drive

## Check-in Instructions

Check-in instructions and badge pick-up details will be communicated to registered attendees
before the event.

## Facility Resources

**Food and Drink**

Light breakfast, lunch, and coffee breaks will be provided on both days.

**Internet**

Wi-Fi will be available at the venue. Connection details will be shared on-site.

## Hotels and Places to Stay

Sunnyvale offers a wide range of accommodation options within walking distance or a short drive
of the Google offices. Recommended hotels and a group rate (if available) will be announced
on the [repo-discuss mailing list](https://groups.google.com/g/repo-discuss).
